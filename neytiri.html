Neytiri

I finished a pencil drawing of Neytiri from Avatar (2009) over a year ago, but I still love how it turned out. Her eyes were the hardest part—I wanted them to feel alive. Shading her markings and skin texture was a challenge, but it was so satisfying to bring her to life on paper. It is one of my favourite works.
